Welcome to Neardle

To play Neardle, enter a real 5 letter word then press enter. The letters will become colored.


H

A

P

P

Y


The letters are colored along a gradient from red to green. The closer to red, the farther away in the alphabet that slot is from the correct letter. The closer to green, the closer it is in the alphabet. If a letter turns blue, it is correct.
